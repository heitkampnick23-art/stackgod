Bring your own developer accounts

Apple Guideline 4.2.6 requires apps to publish under their owner's account, so Stakgod never publishes on your behalf. To ship to TestFlight or the App Store, connect your own Apple Developer account ($99/yr to Apple). For Google Play, connect your Play Console account ($25 one-time to Google). We automate every upload using your keys — your apps, your name on the listing, your revenue. Connect from your dashboard. Keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM.
